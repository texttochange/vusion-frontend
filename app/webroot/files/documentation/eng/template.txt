<p>A template describe the formating messages. It's usually use for localization of open and closed question. The words QUESTION, ANSWERS, ANSWER, KEYWORD and SHORTCODE will be replace when the message is send.</p>

Example Closed Question in english:<br />
<span style="color:red">QUESTION<br />ANSWERS<br /></span>
To Reply, type <span style="color:red">KEYWORD </span>&lt;space&gt;&lt;Answer Nr&gt; &amp;
send to <span style="color:red">SHORTCODE</span>
<br />
